Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a vibrant metropolis blending modern skyscrapers with historic temples, famous for anime, technology, and sushi.

  • Province/State: Tokyo Metropolis
  • Country: Japan
  • Population: ~14 million
  • Latitude & Longitude: 35.6762° N, 139.6503° E

Vancouver, Canada

Vancouver is a coastal city surrounded by mountains, known for its beautiful nature, multicultural vibe, and film industry.

  • Province/State: British Columbia
  • Country: Canada
  • Population: ~2.6 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 49.2827° N, 123.1207° W

Orlando, USA

Orlando, located in central Florida, is famous for its theme parks including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios.

  • Province/State: Florida
  • Country: United States
  • Population: ~316,000 (city)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 28.5383° N, 81.3792° W

Seoul, South Korea

Seoul is a modern and dynamic city that blends cutting-edge technology with rich traditions and historic palaces.

  • Province/State: Seoul Capital Area
  • Country: South Korea
  • Population: ~9.7 million (city), 25 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 37.5665° N, 126.9780° E

Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is known for its art, culture, fashion, and iconic Eiffel Tower.

  • Province/State: Île-de-France
  • Country: France
  • Population: ~11 million (metro)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 48.8566° N, 2.3522° E
